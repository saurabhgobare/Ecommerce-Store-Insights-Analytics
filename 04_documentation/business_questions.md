# Business Questions – Ecommerce Store Analytics

This document lists the **business questions** addressed in the Ecommerce Store – Insights and Analytics project.  
It explains the **purpose** of each query or dashboard and how it helps decision-making.

---

## 1. General Sales Insights
1. **Total Revenue Generated**
   - What is the total revenue from all orders?
   - Helps measure overall business performance.

2. **Revenue Excluding Returned Orders**
   - Revenue only from orders that were not returned.
   - Provides a realistic view of earned revenue.

3. **Total Revenue per Year / Month**
   - Track revenue trends over time.
   - Helps in forecasting and seasonal analysis.

4. **Revenue by Product / Category**
   - Understand which products/categories generate the most revenue.
   - Supports product strategy and inventory planning.

5. **Average Order Value (AOV) Across All Orders**
   - Average spending per order.
   - Indicates customer purchasing behavior.

6. **AOV per Year / Month**
   - Tracks changes in AOV over time.
   - Helps evaluate effectiveness of promotions or pricing.

7. **Average Order Size by Region**
   - Compares order size across different regions.
   - Useful for regional marketing and logistics planning.

---

## 2. Customer Insights
1. **Top 10 Customers by Total Revenue Spent**
   - Identify high-value customers.
   - Supports customer loyalty programs.

2. **Repeat Customer Rate**
   - Percentage of customers placing multiple orders.
   - Indicates customer retention.

3. **Average Time Between Two Consecutive Orders (Region-wise)**
   - Helps understand customer buying cycles in different regions.
   - Supports regional promotions.

4. **Customer Segmentation (Based on Total Spend)**
   - Platinum: > 1500
   - Gold: 1000–1500
   - Silver: 500–999
   - Bronze: < 500
   - Helps target customers for personalized marketing.

5. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**
   - Total revenue a customer contributes over their lifetime.
   - Supports strategic decisions in marketing and retention.

---

## 3. Product & Order Insights
1. **Top 10 Most Sold Products (By Quantity)**
   - Identifies popular products.
   - Guides inventory and supply chain decisions.

2. **Top 10 Most Sold Products (By Revenue)**
   - Shows products generating most revenue.
   - Helps focus on high-value products.

3. **Products with Highest Return Rate**
   - Identifies quality issues or mismatch in expectations.
   - Supports product improvement and customer satisfaction.

4. **Return Rate by Category**
   - Understand which categories have higher returns.
   - Helps in category-level analysis and decisions.

5. **Average Price of Products per Region**
   - Helps compare pricing strategy across regions.
   - Supports regional promotions and pricing adjustments.

6. **Sales Trend for Each Product Category**
   - Tracks popularity and growth of product categories over time.
   - Guides category-focused marketing strategies.

---

## 4. Temporal Trends
1. **Monthly Sales Trends Over the Past Year**
   - Understand seasonal patterns and revenue fluctuations.
   - Helps with planning campaigns and inventory.

2. **Average Order Value (AOV) Change by Month / Week**
   - Tracks purchasing behavior over time.
   - Supports promotions and pricing strategies.

---

## 5. Regional Insights
1. **Regions with Highest and Lowest Order Volume**
   - Highlights strong and weak regions.
   - Supports targeted regional campaigns.

2. **Revenue per Region**
   - Compare revenue performance across regions.
   - Helps allocate marketing and resources efficiently.

---

## 6. Return & Refund Insights
1. **Overall Return Rate by Product Category**
   - Understand which categories are prone to returns.
   - Supports product improvement and quality control.

2. **Overall Return Rate by Region**
   - Identify regions with higher returns.
   - Helps in operational planning and logistics.

3. **Customers Making Frequent Returns**
   - Identify customers with repeated returns.
   - Helps manage risk and optimize return policies.
